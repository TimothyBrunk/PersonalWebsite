A Little About Tim Brunk

After graduating college with a B.A. in Psychology I obtained a position in the health insurance industry. There I spent 5+ years learning key communication and organizational skills as well as helping clients find products that met their needs and adhered to changing government regulations.

As I cultivated new skills I also realized my true passions: technology and information. These, along with my desire to have a fulfilling career led me to pursue web development. After spending some time maximizing efficiencies for an e-commerce start-up and teaching myself to code on the side, I realized that I needed professional training to achieve my goal of developing impactful technologies.

A Full-Stack Web Development Bootcamp was the most efficient and effective path to a career as a web developer. At Skill Distillery we spent nineteen weeks intensively learning and writing full-stack programs in Java and JavaScript using SQL and Mongo databases and many other technologies.

Today I am ecstatic to be in this industry. There is never a day that is routine or boring. The thrill and challenge of solving problems and developing solutions for people is my passion!
